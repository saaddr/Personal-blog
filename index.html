- Story -

Introduction

Hi, my name is Saad Driouech. I’m 24 years old and from Casablanca, Morocco. I’ve always been curious about web development ever since I first heard about it years ago, but I never took the step to learn it. I used to believe it was too complicated and would take at least five years to build a website.

Discovering

Everything changed when a friend introduced me to 1337. They spoke so highly of the program, its unique approach to learning, and the opportunities it offered, and it inspired me to give it a shot. I realized that with the right environment and guidance, and that was the missing piece.

Achievement

After learning about the 42 network and its philosophy, I was determined to join the 1337 family. I put in the effort, worked hard, and finally, I did it. Becoming part of this amazing community has been a life-changing experience, and I’m excited to keep learning here!
